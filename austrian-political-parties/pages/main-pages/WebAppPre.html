Tumble Rumble

Basic Explanation

Tumble Rumble is a political inspired brawling game. After choosing a character for each player as well as a map you have to push the opponent off the platform using basic attacks. Score a kill to win a round. Win 3 rounds to win the match. After each round each player can upgrade his characterÂ´s health regeneration, shield, knockback and damage stat. Each character has a unique visual design and unique abilities. Good luck and have fun!

Controls

(Player 1) left: a, right: d, jump: w, attack: q, active: e, ultimate: r
(Player 2) left: j, right: l, jump: i, attack: o, active: u, ultimate: z
