Tumble Rumble

Click the Link and play our fighting game

Tumbel Rumble is a selfmade fighting game. It was developed by Romeo Bhuiyan , Zeno Paukner, Benjamin Imsirovic. Just click the button and try it with a friend. You can play it with just one Keyboard. One player controlls his character with: w a s d and the other character with: j i o l. The game is over if one of the two character fall down the platform. Just try it and you will have a lot of fun.
